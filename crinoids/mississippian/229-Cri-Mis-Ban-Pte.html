Pterotocrinus depressus

 • Mississippian
• Bangor Formation
• Franklin County, Alabama, USA

Size: 3 cm across the "wings"

Pterotocrinus is a very unusual crinoid aptly named for the striking "wings" arising from the tegmen ("Ptero-" deriving from the Greek word for "wing"). Different species of Pterotocrinus are often distinguished and named by the shape of these wings.
