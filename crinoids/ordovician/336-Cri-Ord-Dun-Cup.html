Cupulocrinus crossmani

 • Ordovician
• Dunleith Formation, Galena Group
• Iowa or Minnesota, USA

Size: 2.5 cm across the arms

Identification based on "Brower, J. 1992. Cupulocrinid Crinoids from the Middle Ordovician (Galena Group, Dunleith Formation) of Northern Iowa and Southern Minnesota"; confirmed by William Ausich.
