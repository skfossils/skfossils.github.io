Asteroid
Promopaleaster pricei

 Ordovician
Martinsburg Formation
Swatara Gap, Pennsylvania, USA

Size: 4.5 across the arms

This is an impressive starfish from the famed Martinsburg Formation of Swatara Gap. According to a long time collector of this locality, "P. pricei" is one of the rarest starfish and rarest fossils period from Swatara Gap, with only a handful of specimens known. I believe the "spines" around the arms are the specimen's remarkably-preserved tube feet.
