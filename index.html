Samuel Kim Fossil Collection

I am an avid fossil collector and this website showcases the finest and most interesting/unusual fossils in my collection. I primarily focus on crinoids, which in my opinion are the most beautiful and fascinatingly-variable group of invertebrates throughout the fossil record. I took heavy inspiration (with permission) from http://www.crinus.info, the website of Joseph Koniecki; my fossil mentor and good friend who possesses a world-class fossil collection that truly sparked my obsession with crinoids.

I will be updating and adding content from time-to-time, so check back to see new specimens and descriptions.

Feel free to contact me if you see any specimens that you have comments on or are curious about, or if you have any interesting crinoid fossils you might be willing to part with.
